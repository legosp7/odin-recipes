Fried Rice

A nice taste of Chinese food, quick and easy to make with leftover rice!

Ingredients

  • At least a day old rice, chilled is best
  • Butter or neutral oil
  • 2 large eggs, whisked
  • 1 small white onion
  • 1/2 cup of frozen peas
  • 3-4 tablespoons soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Add the butter or oil to a pan, heating it over medium-high heat
  2. Add in the veggies, and saute for about five minutes
  3. Increase heat to high, add rice and soy sauce, sauteing for another 3 minutes or so. Stir occassionally
  4. Add in eggs and stir to combine
  5. Serve
