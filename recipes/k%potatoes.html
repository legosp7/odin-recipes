Korean Braised Potatoes

Korean braised potatoes are an easy recipe that taste extremly savory and flavorful! They are quick and easy to cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb potato, sliced and diced
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2/3 cups of water
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

Instructions

  1. Mix all the liquids together except the oils
  2. Heat the oil and sautee the potatoes for 4-5 minutes until golden
  3. Add the sauce mixture, simmer for an additional 5-6 minutes
  4. Add sesame oil and seeds
  5. Done! Plate and enjoy!
